STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links sent via email or messages.
- Always verify requests for money, even if they come from known contacts.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making payments.
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal information with anyone.

UPI / FINANCIAL SAFETY:
- Always double-check the UPI ID or phone number before sending money.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept unexpected payment requests.

REPORTING:
- Report cyber fraud immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud cases.
