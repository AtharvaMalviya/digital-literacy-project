SOCIAL MEDIA DO’s AND DON’Ts

DO’s:
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile.
- Think before posting anything online.
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.
- Share useful, positive, and meaningful information.
- Verify facts before sharing any news or information.

DON’Ts:
- Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not spread misinformation or fake news.
- Avoid engaging in unnecessary arguments or online fights.
- Do not post anything that could harm your reputation in the future.
